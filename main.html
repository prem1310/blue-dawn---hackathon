Hindi

Basic conversational skills might take 3-6 months (300+ hours). Achieving fluency in hindi often needs a year or more of consistent learning,depending on individual learning methods.

Chinese

Learning Chinese varies widely due to complexity. Basic conversational skills might take 6-12 months (600-1200 hours). Fluency demands years of dedicated study and immersion.

Arabic

Arabic learning time varies. Basic grasp might take 6-12 months (600+ hours). Achieving fluency can span several years with dedicated practice.

German

Basic German can take 3-6 months (300+ hours), while fluency demands a year or more with dedicated learning and practice.

Spanish

Basic Spanish can take 3-6 months (150-300 hours). Achieving fluency may need a year or more of regular practice and immersion.

Japanese

Basic Japanese takes 1-2 years (600-1,200 hours). Proficiency varies; mastering kanji and nuances can extend learning beyond 3 years.
